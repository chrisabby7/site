Hey there! I'm Chrisma, and I'm so happy you've stopped by. I'm an aspiring Nepali singer-songwriter based in Virginia. Singing has always been an integral part of my life—it’s given me a sense of belonging, purpose, friendships, and connections. Now, at 27, I’m finally ready to share my own songs with the world, a dream I’ve held very close to my heart for a really long time.

So, I invite you to join me as I begin this new journey. The songs I’ll be sharing come straight from the archives of my heart, and I hope the feelings and stories I’ve poured into them resonate with your own.
Much love, and thank you! ❤️
